# SWOT Analysis

## Strengths
- Established relationships with doctors
- Niche medical equipment market
- Demonstration-based trust building
- Existing revenue (~₹5 Cr)

## Weaknesses
- No digital presence
- Manual customer record management
- Sales dependent on one person
- Limited demonstration capacity

## Opportunities
- Online product demonstrations
- Website for lead generation
- CRM for customer management
- Expansion to other states

## Threats
- Competitors reaching doctors faster
- Sales employees leaving and starting their own business
- Customers switching to other suppliers
